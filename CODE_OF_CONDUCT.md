# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant principles in a concise form.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Focus criticism on technical evidence and project safety.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and correct them promptly.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Pressuring contributors to share credentials, private data, or financial records.
- Presenting unverified trading claims as investment advice.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior harms contributors or the safety goals of the project.

## Reporting

Open a GitHub issue for conduct concerns that can be handled publicly. For sensitive concerns, open a minimal issue asking for a private contact path without including private details.
